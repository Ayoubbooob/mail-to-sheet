About

What is Email Exporter for Google Sheets™?

Email Exporter for Google Sheets™ is a Google Workspace™ add-on designed for professionals, teams, and small businesses who need to organize email data efficiently. It connects your Gmail™ account to Google Sheets™ and automatically exports email metadata — saving you hours of manual data entry. Boost your productivity, keep your data organized, and automate repetitive tasks.

Features

Everything You Need

Powerful email export capabilities, zero complexity.

Export Emails Automatically

Extract email metadata from Gmail™ and send it directly to Google Sheets™ with one click.

Filter by Label / Sender / Date

Narrow down your export using powerful filters — by Gmail™ label, sender address, or date range.

Append Data to Google Sheets™

Exported data is appended to your spreadsheet — keeping your existing data safe and organized.

Easy & Secure Installation

Install directly from the Google Workspace Marketplace. Secure OAuth authentication — no passwords stored.

Security & Trust

Built with Privacy in Mind

Your data security is our top priority.

Google OAuth Authentication

The add-on authenticates using Google's secure OAuth 2.0 protocol. We never see or store your password.

Minimal Permissions

We only request the OAuth scopes strictly necessary to read email metadata and write to your spreadsheet.

No Data Storage

Email data is processed in real-time and written directly to your Google Sheets™. We do not store your data on external servers.

Google Workspace™ Integration

The add-on integrates directly with core Google Workspace™ services APIs — Gmail™ API and Google Sheets™ API.
