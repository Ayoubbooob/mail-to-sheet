Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Email Exporter for Google Sheets™.

  1. Open a Google Sheets™ spreadsheet.
  2. Go to Extensions > Add-ons > Get add-ons.
  3. Search for "Email Exporter for Google Sheets™".
  4. Click Install and follow the prompts to authorize the add-on.

Alternatively, you can install it directly from the Google Workspace Marketplace.

When you first run the add-on, Google will prompt you to authorize the required permissions. You'll see a consent screen explaining what data the add-on can access:

  • Gmail™ read access — to read email metadata for exports.
  • Google Sheets™ access — to write exported data to your spreadsheet.

Click "Allow" to grant permissions. The add-on uses Google's secure OAuth 2.0 protocol — your password is never shared with us.

  1. Open a Google Sheets™ spreadsheet.
  2. Go to Extensions > Email Exporter for Google Sheets™ > Open.
  3. In the sidebar, configure your filters (label, sender, date range).
  4. Click "Export".
  5. The email metadata will be appended to your active sheet.

If you encounter authorization errors, try the following steps:

  1. Revoke and re-authorize: Go to Google Account Permissions, find "Email Exporter for Google Sheets™", click Remove Access, then re-open the add-on to authorize again.
  2. Check your browser: Ensure pop-ups are not blocked, as the authorization window may open in a popup.
  3. Clear cache: Try clearing your browser's cache and cookies, then attempt authorization again.
  4. Use a supported browser: The add-on works best in Google Chrome.

If the issue persists, contact us at aeob2090@gmail.com.
